# InterBankHub - Getting Started Guide

## Welcome to InterBankHub

InterBankHub is your unified platform for creating and managing bank accounts in India, USA, and UK. This guide will walk you through your first steps.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

### What You'll Need:
- A valid email address
- A strong password (min 8 characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
- Your phone number
- Your country (India, USA, or UK)
- Know if you're signing up as a User or Admin

### How to Sign Up as a User:

1. **Visit the home page** and click "Sign Up"
2. **Choose "User" as your account type**
3. **Fill in your details:**
   - Email address
   - Password (strong password recommended)
   - Confirm password
   - Username
   - Phone number
   - Country (India, USA, or UK)
4. **Click "Sign Up"**
5. **You'll be redirected to sign in**
6. **Enter your email and password to log in**

**Time needed:** 3-5 minutes

---

### How to Sign Up as an Admin (Bank Staff):

1. **Visit the home page** and click "Sign Up"
2. **Choose "Admin" as your account type**
3. **Fill in your details:**
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Confirm password
   - Username
   - Phone number
   - Select your bank name from the list
   - Select your country
4. **Click "Sign Up"**
5. **You'll be redirected to the Admin Dashboard**

**Time needed:** 3-5 minutes

---

## Step 2: Log In to Your Account

1. **Click "Sign In"** on the home page
2. **Enter your email address** you used during signup
3. **Enter your password**
4. **Click "Sign In"**
5. **You'll be taken to your dashboard** (User Page or Admin Dashboard)

---

## Step 3: For Users - Explore and Create Your First Account

### View Available Banks:

1. **Click "Browse Banks"** from your dashboard
2. **Select your country** (India, USA, or UK)
3. **Search by:**
   - Bank name (use the search box)
   - City (use the city filter dropdown)
4. **Click on a bank** to see details
5. **Click "Create Account"** to start the account creation process

### Create Your First Bank Account:

1. **From Browse Banks, click "Create Account"**
2. **You'll see a 6-step form:**

**Step 1: Select Branch**
- Choose your branch from the dropdown
- Click "Next"

**Step 2: Personal Details**
- Fill in your full name, date of birth, gender
- Enter your contact information
- Enter required ID numbers (Aadhaar/PAN for India, SSN for USA, NIN for UK)
- Click "Next"

**Step 3: Educational Details**
- Select your education level
- Enter your institution name
- Enter your course/degree
- Enter completion year
- Click "Next"

**Step 4: Income Details**
- Select your employment status
- Enter occupation
- Enter monthly and annual income
- Select income source
- Click "Next"

**Step 5: Nominee Details**
- Enter nominee's full name
- Select relationship
- Enter nominee's date of birth and contact
- Click "Next"

**Step 6: Documents & Account Type**
- Upload documents:
  - ID Proof
  - Address Proof
  - Income Proof
  - Passport Photo
- Select your account type (Savings, Current, etc.)
- Enter initial deposit amount
- Check consent checkbox
- Click "Submit"

**Time needed:** 15-20 minutes

---

## Step 4: Manage Your Profile

1. **Click "Your Page"** from your dashboard
2. **View your profile information:**
   - Username
   - Email
   - Phone number
3. **To edit your profile:**
   - Click the "Edit" button
   - Update your information
   - Click "Save Changes"

4. **To change your password:**
   - Look for "Change Password" option
   - Enter your current password
   - Enter your new password
   - Confirm new password
   - Click "Update"

---

## Step 5: View Your Bank Accounts

1. **Go to "Your Page"**
2. **You'll see all your linked bank accounts**
3. **Filter by status:**
   - All (all accounts)
   - Pending (under approval)
   - Approved (ready to use)
   - Rejected (application denied)
4. **Click on any account** to see full details
5. **View or download documents** from your account

---

## For Admin Users - Your First Steps

### View Pending Applications:

1. **Log in to your Admin account**
2. **You'll see the Admin Dashboard**
3. **View "Pending" applications** - these are accounts waiting for approval
4. **Click on an application** to see full details:
   - Applicant's personal information
   - Educational details
   - Income information
   - Nominee details
   - Uploaded documents

### Review and Approve/Reject:

1. **Review all details and documents**
2. **Click "Approve"** if everything is correct
3. **Click "Reject"** if there are issues
4. **Applicants receive notifications** about their application status

### Manage Your Admin Profile:

1. **Go to your Admin Dashboard**
2. **Click "Profile" section**
3. **Edit your information:**
   - Username
   - Email
4. **Change your password** if needed

## Common Questions

### "What should I do after I submit my account application?"
- Check your account status regularly in "Your Page" (typically 2-5 business days)
- Your status will update automatically when admin reviews it
- If approved, you can start using your account
- If rejected, you'll see the details in your dashboard

### "I can't find my bank"
- Make sure you selected the correct country
- Try searching by exact bank name
- Check if we support your bank
- Contact support if issues persist

### "How long does account approval take?"
- Typical time: 2-5 business days
- Time varies depending on bank processing
- Check your dashboard regularly for status updates
- Status updates automatically when admin makes a decision

### "Can I create multiple accounts?"
- Yes, you can create accounts with different banks
- You can also create accounts in different countries
- All accounts appear in your "Your Page"

### "I forgot my password"
- Contact support at support@interbankshub.com
- Provide your email address
- They'll help you reset your password
