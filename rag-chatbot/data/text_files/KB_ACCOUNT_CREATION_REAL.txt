# InterBankHub - Account Creation by Country

Complete step-by-step guide for creating bank accounts in India, USA, and UK.

---

## 🇮🇳 India - Account Creation

### Account Types Available:
- **Savings Account** - For personal saving
- **Current Account** - For business transactions
- **Salary Account** - Special account for salaried employees
- **Fixed Deposit** - For fixed returns on savings

### Form Fields by Section:

**Step 1: Select Branch**
- Choose your bank branch from the dropdown

**Step 2: Personal Details**
- Full Name (as per ID)
- Date of Birth
- Gender (Male/Female/Other)
- Mobile Number
- Email Address
- Address (complete residential address)
- Aadhaar Number (12-digit ID)
- PAN Number (10-digit tax ID)

**Step 3: Educational Details**
- Education Level (High School, Diploma, Graduate, Post Graduate, Professional)
- Institution Name
- Course/Degree
- Year of Completion
- Percentage/CGPA (optional)

**Step 4: Income Details**
- Employment Status (Employed/Self-Employed/Business/Student/Retired/Unemployed)
- Employer Name (if applicable)
- Occupation
- Monthly Income (in INR)
- Annual Income (in INR)
- Income Source (Salary/Business/Investment/Pension/Other)

**Step 5: Nominee Details**
- Nominee Full Name
- Relationship with Nominee (Spouse/Father/Mother/Son/Daughter/Brother/Sister/Other)
- Nominee Date of Birth
- Nominee Contact Number
- Nominee Address

**Step 6: Documents & Account**
- **Upload Files:**
  - ID Proof (Aadhaar, Passport, License, etc.)
  - Address Proof (Utility bill, Government letter, etc.)
  - Income Proof (Salary slip, Tax return, etc.)
  - Passport Photo
  - Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, PDF
  - Max file size: 5MB recommended
- **Account Information:**
  - Account Type (Savings/Current/Salary/Fixed Deposit)
  - Initial Deposit Amount (in INR)
  - Consent Checkbox (I agree to terms and conditions)

### Processing Timeline:
- Document submission: Instant
- Bank verification: 1-3 business days
- Approval/Rejection: Status updates in dashboard
- Total: 2-5 business days typical

### Tips for India Account:
- Aadhaar must be valid 12-digit number
- PAN format: e.g., AAAAA1234A
- Address should match your ID
- Documents must be clear and readable
- Income figures should be realistic

## Document Upload Guidelines

### General Requirements for All Countries:

1. **Clear and Readable**
   - Good lighting in photos
   - Entire document visible
   - No blurry or cropped images

2. **File Format**
   - Accepted: JPG, JPEG, PNG, PDF
   - Avoid: BMP, GIF, TIFF

3. **File Size**
   - Maximum: 5MB per file recommended
   - Compress if larger

4. **Color**
   - Use color photos (not black & white)
   - Clear contrast for text

5. **Currency**
   - All documents should be in English
   - For India: Original or certified copies
   - For USA: Government-issued documents
   - For UK: Recent documents

### Each Document Type:

**ID Proof:**
- Front and back clearly visible
- Photo and signature visible
- Not expired
- Matches your name exactly

**Address Proof:**
- Shows your full name
- Shows complete address
- Recent (within 3-6 months)
- From government or utility company

**Income Proof:**
- Shows your name and company
- Shows income amount
- Recent (within last 3 months)
- Clear and readable

**Passport Photo:**
- Smile or neutral expression
- Plain background
- Face clearly visible
- Recent photo

## What Happens After Submission?

### Timeline:

**Day 1:**
- Your application is submitted
- Documents are received and verified for format
- System confirms receipt

**Day 2-3:**
- Bank reviews your documents
- Verifies personal information
- May request additional details if needed

**Day 4-5:**
- Final approval decision made
- Status updated in your account dashboard
- Check status to see approval or rejection

**After Approval:**
- You can start using your account
- Access account details
- Make transactions (if supported)
- Link to other platforms if available

### If Rejected:

- Check your dashboard for rejection reason
- Common reasons:
  - Unclear documents
  - Information mismatch
  - Missing information
  - Document requirements not met
- You can resubmit with corrections

## Troubleshooting

**"My documents won't upload"**
- Check file format (JPG, JPEG, PNG, PDF only)
- Check file size (max 5MB)
- Try different browser
- Clear browser cache and retry

**"I made a mistake in the form"**
- Contact support: support@interbankshub.com
- Provide your email and account details
- They can help correct information

**"My application was rejected"**
- Check the rejection reason email
- Resubmit with corrected information
- Ensure all documents are current and valid
- Contact support if you need clarification

**"How long should I wait?"**
- Typical: 2-5 business days
- Not counting weekends/holidays
- Check "Your Page" dashboard regularly to see current status
- Status updates automatically when admin makes decision
