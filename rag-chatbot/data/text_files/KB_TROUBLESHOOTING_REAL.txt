# InterBankHub - Troubleshooting Guide

Solutions for common issues on InterBankHub.

---

## Account & Login Issues

### "I can't log in"

**Symptom:** Login button doesn't work or shows error

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your credentials**
   - Verify email is correct
   - Verify password is correct
   - Password is case-sensitive
   - Check Caps Lock

2. **Try these steps:**
   - Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
   - Clear browser cookies:
     - Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
     - Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Clear Data
   - Try a different browser
   - Try from a different network

3. **If you forgot your password:**
   - Contact support: support@interbankshub.com
   - They'll help you reset it
   - Include your registered email address

**If still not working:** 
- Email support@interbankshub.com with:
  - Your email address
  - Error message you see
  - Screenshot of the problem

---

### "I forgot my password"

**Solution:**
1. On Sign In page, look for "Forgot Password" link (if available)
2. If not available, contact support:
   - Email: support@interbankshub.com
   - Include your registered email
   - Support will help you reset
   - Response time: 24-48 hours

---

### "I can't sign up"

**Problem: Form won't submit**

**Check these:**
- All required fields are filled in red/marked as required
- Email is valid format (example@domain.com)
- Password meets requirements (8+ characters, letters, numbers)
- Passwords match in both fields
- Select your country

**If form still won't submit:**
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache
- Disable browser extensions (some interfere with forms)
- Try a different device

---

### "My account is locked"

**Symptom:** "Account locked" or "Too many attempts" message

**Reason:** Too many failed login attempts

**Solution:**
1. Wait 30 minutes
2. Try logging in again
3. If still locked, contact support:
   - Email: support@interbankshub.com
   - They can unlock your account

---

## Password & Security Issues

### "I want to change my password"

**Step 1: Log in**
- Sign in with your current credentials

**Step 2: Go to profile**
- Click "Your Page" (Users) or Admin Dashboard

**Step 3: Find password option**
- Look for "Change Password" or "Security" section

**Step 4: Update**
- Enter current password
- Enter new password (must be different)
- Confirm new password
- Click "Update"

---

### "I'm getting logged out unexpectedly"

**Reason:** Session timeout or browser issue

**Solutions:**
1. **Clear browser cache:**
   - Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
   - Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Clear Data
   - Safari: Develop → Empty Caches

2. **Try these:**
   - Log out completely
   - Close all browser tabs
   - Restart browser
   - Log in again

3. **Check your browser:**
   - Update browser to latest version
   - Try a different browser
   - Check if cookies are enabled

---

## Profile & Account Management Issues

### "I can't edit my profile"

**Problem: Edit button doesn't work or fields won't update**

**Solutions:**
1. **Check permissions:**
   - Are you logged in?
   - Are you on "Your Page" (Users)?
   - Are you on Admin Dashboard (Admins)?

2. **Try these:**
   - Refresh page (Ctrl+R)
   - Log out and log in again
   - Try a different browser
   - Wait a moment and try again (may be loading)

3. **Check your information:**
   - All fields filled correctly?
   - Email is valid format?
   - Phone number is valid?

**If still not working:**
- Email support@interbankshub.com

---

### "I can't download my documents"

**Problem: Download button doesn't work or file won't download**

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your browser:**
   - Is download enabled in browser settings?
   - Check your Downloads folder
   - Try a different browser

2. **Refresh and retry:**
   - Refresh the page
   - Wait for page to load completely
   - Try clicking download again

3. **Check internet:**
   - Is your connection stable?
   - Try on a different network
   - Disable VPN if using one

4. **Try different document:**
   - Sometimes one document works better than others
   - Try downloading another document first
   - If that works, try original document again

---

### "My account status shows 'Pending' but I submitted long ago"

**Reason:** Processing delays

**Solutions:**
1. **Check email:**
   - Look for status update emails
   - Check spam/junk folder

2. **Normal timeline:**
   - Typical: 2-5 business days
   - May be longer depending on volume
   - Does not count weekends/holidays

3. **Contact admin:**
   - Email your bank's support
   - Ask for status update
   - Provide your email and account details

## Document Upload Issues

### "I can't upload documents"

**Problem: Upload button doesn't work**

**Check these:**
1. **File format:**
   - Supported: JPG, JPEG, PNG, PDF
   - NOT supported: BMP, GIF, TIFF, DOC
   - Convert if needed (use online converter if necessary)

2. **File size:**
   - Maximum: 5MB per file
   - If larger, compress the file
   - Use compression tool or upload service

3. **Browser:**
   - Try a different browser
   - Update your browser
   - Disable extensions temporarily

4. **Internet:**
   - Check connection is stable
   - Try uploading on better WiFi
   - Mobile data might be slower

---

### "My documents are rejected for being unclear"

**Problem: After uploading, documents are said to be unclear**

**Solution:** Reupload with clear documents

**Take better photos:**
- Good lighting (daylight is best)
- Entire document visible (all edges)
- No shadows or glare
- Document laying flat
- Color photo (not black & white)
- Not cropped or partial

**Convert properly:**
- Use phone camera app, not just screenshot
- If scanning, use quality scanner
- If using PDF, ensure text is readable
- Check preview before uploading

**Compress without losing quality:**
- Use online image compressor
- Check file opens clearly before uploading
- If text is blurry, use higher quality

---

### "I uploaded wrong document"

**Solution:**
1. If form not yet submitted:
   - Delete the document (if delete option available)
   - Upload correct document
   - Then submit form

2. If already submitted:
   - Contact support@interbankshub.com
   - Explain which document is wrong
   - They'll advise on resubmission

## Bank & Account Creation Issues

### "I can't find my bank"

**Problem: Bank not appearing in search or list**

**Check these:**
1. **Did you select correct country?**
   - Your bank must be in selected country
   - Switch country dropdown if needed
   - Different countries have different banks

2. **Try searching:**
   - Use exact bank name
   - Try spelling variations (State Bank vs SBI)
   - Try with/without words (Bank of India vs Bank India)

3. **Search by city:**
   - Use city filter
   - Select your city
   - See if bank has branch there

**If bank still not found:**
- We may not partner with that bank yet
- Contact support: support@interbankshub.com
- They can suggest alternatives
- Or tell us about banks you'd like added

---

### "I can't see branches for my selected bank"

**Problem: Branch dropdown empty or showing no options**

**Solutions:**
1. **Select bank first:**
   - Choose bank from list
   - Then select city
   - Then branches should appear

2. **Try refreshing:**
   - Refresh the page
   - Try again
   - Wait a few seconds for page to load

3. **Check selection:**
   - Ensure country is selected
   - Ensure bank is selected
   - Ensure city is selected

**If no branches appear:**
- Contact support
- Bank might not have branches in your city yet
- You can try other banks

---

### "Account creation form won't submit"

**Problem: Submit button doesn't work or shows error**

**Check these:**
1. **All fields completed:**
   - Look for red asterisks * (required fields)
   - Fill all required fields
   - No blank fields in any step

2. **Information accurate:**
   - Check spelling of names
   - Verify dates (DOB format)
   - Check ID numbers are valid format:
     - India: 12-digit Aadhaar, 10-char PAN
     - USA: 9-digit SSN (XXX-XX-XXXX)
     - UK: NIN format (2 letters, 6 digits, 1 letter)

3. **Documents uploaded:**
   - All 4 documents needed
   - All documents clear and readable
   - All file sizes under 5MB
   - All files in correct format (JPG, PNG, PDF)

4. **Try these:**
   - Refresh page and try again
   - Try different browser
   - Disable browser extensions
   - Clear browser cache

---

### "I made a mistake in my application"

**If not yet submitted:**
- Go back to that step
- Correct the information
- Review before resubmitting

**If already submitted:**
- Contact support@interbankshub.com
- Explain what's wrong
- They'll advise on what to do:
  - May allow correction
  - May need to resubmit
  - May automatically update

## Browsing & Navigation Issues

### "I can't browse banks"

**Problem: Browse Banks page not loading or shows error**

**Solutions:**
1. **Refresh page:**
   - Click refresh (Ctrl+R)
   - Wait for page to load
   - Try again

2. **Check login:**
   - Are you logged in as a user?
   - Users can browse banks
   - Admins cannot browse banks
   - Try logging in again

3. **Try different browser:**
   - Switch to Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
   - Clear cache and try

4. **Check internet:**
   - Stable connection?
   - Try different network
   - Disable VPN

---

### "Page won't load"

**Problem: Blank page or loading forever**

**Solutions:**
1. **Refresh page:**
   - Press Ctrl+R (Windows) or Cmd+R (Mac)
   - Wait 10 seconds
   - Try again

2. **Clear cache:**
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Restart browser
   - Try again

3. **Try different browser:**
   - Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari
   - Sometimes one works better

4. **Check internet:**
   - Is WiFi/data connected?
   - Try different network
   - Restart router

---

### "Buttons don't work"

**Problem: Clicking buttons does nothing**

**Solutions:**
1. **Try double-clicking** (sometimes single click doesn't register)

2. **Refresh page** (Ctrl+R) and try again

3. **Wait for loading:**
   - Page might be loading
   - Wait a few seconds
   - Try again

4. **Try different browser**

5. **Disable extensions:**
   - Browser extensions might interfere
   - Disable them temporarily
   - Try again

---

## Admin-Specific Issues

### "I can't see pending applications"

**Problem: Application list is empty**

**Solutions:**
1. **Check status filter:**
   - Are you on "Pending" tab?
   - Click "Pending" to see pending apps
   - May be showing "Approved" or "Rejected" instead

2. **Wait for applications:**
   - If just set up, no applications yet
   - Applications come from users
   - May take time to receive applications

3. **Refresh:**
   - Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
   - New applications may have arrived

---

### "I can't approve/reject applications"

**Problem: Buttons don't work**

**Solutions:**
1. **Check button visibility:**
   - Scroll down in application details
   - Buttons at bottom
   - May need to scroll to see

2. **Refresh and try:**
   - Refresh page
   - Click application again
   - Try button again

3. **Check login:**
   - Are you logged in as admin?
   - Users can't approve/reject
   - Log out and log in as admin

---

## Theme & Display Issues

### "Dark/Light theme not working"

**Problem: Theme toggle doesn't work or reverts**

**Solutions:**
1. **Click theme button:**
   - Look for sun/moon icon
   - Click to toggle between dark and light
   - Page should change colors

2. **Refresh:**
   - If reverted after refresh
   - Click theme button again
   - Refresh page
   - Theme should persist

3. **Clear cache:**
   - Clear browser cache
   - Try toggling theme again

---

### "Page looks broken or text is cut off"

**Problem: Display issues**

**Solutions:**
1. **Check zoom level:**
   - Press Ctrl+0 (Cmd+0 on Mac) to reset zoom
   - Page might be zoomed too much

2. **Refresh:**
   - Press Ctrl+R (Cmd+R)
   - Wait for page to fully load

3. **Try different browser:**
   - Some browsers display better than others

4. **Check screen size:**
   - Might be display issue on small screens
   - Try full screen
   - Try different device

## Still Having Issues?

### Contact Support

**Email:** support@interbankshub.com

**Include in your email:**
1. Your registered email address
2. Description of the problem
3. Steps you tried
4. Error message (if any)
5. Screenshots if helpful
6. Your browser and device (Chrome on Windows, etc.)
7. When problem started

**Response time:** 24-48 hours

---

### Helpful Information to Provide:

When contacting support, tell them:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- What error message appeared (screenshot)
- Your browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)
- Your device (Windows, Mac, iPhone, Android)
- Whether it worked before
- If other users have the same problem

---

### Before Contacting Support:

✓ Refresh the page
✓ Try another browser
✓ Clear browser cache
✓ Try from different network
✓ Check this troubleshooting guide
✓ Wait 10 minutes and try again
