## Getting Started

### Sign Up as a User

1. Visit the home page and click **"Sign Up"**
2. Select **"User"** as your account type
3. Fill in your details:
   - Email address (will be your login username)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters, mix of letters, numbers, and symbols recommended)
   - Confirm password
   - Username (display name)
   - Phone number
   - Country (India, USA, or UK)
4. Click **"Sign Up"**
5. You'll be redirected to the sign-in page
6. Log in with your email and password

**Time required:** 3-5 minutes

### First Time Login

1. Go to **Sign In** page
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be taken to your dashboard (**Your Page**)

## User Account Management

### Accessing Your Dashboard

- After login, you'll see your dashboard (**Your Page**) with sidebar navigation:
  - **My Profile** - View and edit user information
  - **Bank Accounts** - View all your created accounts
   - **New Account** - Select bank and create accounts
  - **Support** - Contact support
  - **Help Center** - View frequently asked questions
  - Theme toggle (dark/light mode in top right)

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click **"My Profile"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see your profile information:
   - Username (display name)
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Account status (Active)
   - Member since date

### Editing Your Profile

1. From the **My Profile** section, click **"Edit Details"** button
2. A modal window opens with your profile information
3. Update any of these fields:
   - Username (display name)
   - Email address
   - Phone number
4. Click **"Save"** or **"Save Changes"** button
5. You'll see a success confirmation message
6. Modal will close automatically after 2 seconds

**Validation Requirements:**
- Username must be 3-50 characters
- Email must be a valid email address
- Email must be unique (not already in use)
- Phone number must be valid for your country

### Changing Your Password

1. From the **My Profile** section, click **"Change Password"** button
2. A modal window opens with password fields
3. Enter your **Current Password** (for verification)
4. Enter your **New Password**
5. Confirm your **New Password**
6. Click **"Update"** button
7. You'll see a success confirmation message
8. Modal will close automatically

**Validation Rules:**
- Current password is required for verification
- New password must be different from current password
- New password must be at least 8 characters
- Must include mix of uppercase, lowercase, and numbers

### Toggling Dark/Light Mode

1. Look for the **sun/moon icon** in the top right corner
2. Click **☀️** (sun) for light mode or **🌙** (moon) for dark mode
3. Theme toggles immediately
4. Your preference is automatically saved in your browser
5. Persists across sessions and logins

### Logging Out

1. From the **My Profile** section, click **"Logout"** button
2. Or click the logout icon in the sidebar header
3. Your session will be completely cleared
4. You'll be redirected to the sign-in page
5. Always log out on shared or public computers for security

## Selecting Banks & Creating Accounts

### Accessing Bank Selection

1. From your dashboard, click **"New Account"** in the sidebar
2. Use the built-in bank selection controls on that section

### Bank Selection Interface

**Step 1: Select Country**
- Use the country dropdown
- Options: India, USA, UK
- Banks will load for your selected country

**Step 2: Select City**
- Use the city dropdown to filter banks by location
- Only shows cities with banks in your selected country
- Filters the available banks

**Step 3: Select Bank**
- Use the bank dropdown to see available banks
- Each bank shows if you already have an account with it
- Icon shows: **(Account Exists)** if duplicate found
- Prevents duplicate accounts with same bank

**Step 4: Review Your Selection**
- Summary shows:
  - Selected Country
  - Selected City
  - Selected Bank
  - Warning if account already exists (⚠️)

### Starting Account Creation

1. After selecting bank and city, click **"Create Account with [Bank Name]"**
2. You'll be taken to the 6-step account creation form
3. If you already have an account with that bank, you'll see an error message
4. Select a different bank to proceed

## Creating Bank Accounts

### Starting the Account Creation Process

1. From dashboard, click **"New Account"** in the sidebar
2. Select your preferred Country, City, and Bank
3. Click **"Create Account with [Bank Name]"** button
4. You'll be redirected to the 6-step form

### 6-Step Account Creation Form

The account creation process is split into 6 steps. You must complete each step before proceeding to the next.

#### **Step 1: Select Branch**

- Choose your bank branch from the dropdown
- Only branches for your selected bank are shown
- Click **"Next"** to proceed

#### **Step 2: Personal Details**

**Required fields:**
- Full Name (as it appears on your ID)
- Date of Birth (select from date picker)
- Gender (Male/Female/Other)
- Mobile Number
- Email Address
- Complete Address
- Country-specific ID numbers:
  - **India:** Aadhaar Number (12 digits) + PAN (10 digits)
  - **USA:** Social Security Number (SSN format: XXX-XX-XXXX)
  - **UK:** National Insurance Number (format: AB123456C)

Click **"Next"** when complete.

#### **Step 3: Educational Details**

**Required fields:**
- Education Level (dropdown with country-specific options)
- Institution Name
- Course/Degree Name
- Year of Completion
- Grade/CGPA (optional)

Click **"Next"**.

#### **Step 4: Income Details**

**Required fields:**
- Employment Status:
  - Employed
  - Self-Employed
  - Business Owner
  - Student
  - Retired
  - Unemployed
- Employer Name (if applicable)
- Occupation
- Monthly Income (in local currency)
- Annual Income (in local currency)
- Income Source (Salary, Business, Investment, Pension, Other)

Click **"Next"**.

#### **Step 5: Nominee Details**

**Required fields:**
- Nominee Full Name
- Relationship to You (Spouse, Father, Mother, Son, Daughter, Brother, Sister, Other)
- Nominee Date of Birth
- Nominee Contact Number
- Nominee Address

Click **"Next"**.

#### **Step 6: Documents & Account**

**Upload Documents (all required):**
1. **ID Proof:**
   - National ID, Passport, Driver's License, or Government ID
   - Formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, PDF
   - Max file size: 5MB per document
   - Must be clear and readable

2. **Address Proof:**
   - Utility bill, Government letter, Bank statement, or Lease agreement
   - Same format requirements

3. **Income Proof:**
   - Salary slip, Tax return, Bank statement, or Business statement
   - Same format requirements

4. **Passport Photo:**
   - Recent color photo
   - Same format requirements

**Account Selection:**
- Select account type (varies by country):
  - **India:** Savings, Current, Salary, Fixed Deposit
  - **USA:** Checking, Savings, Money Market, Certificate of Deposit
  - **UK:** Current, Savings, ISA, Fixed Term

**Initial Deposit:**
- Enter the amount you want to deposit initially
- Currency depends on country (INR, USD, GBP)

**Consent:**
- Check the box: "I agree to terms and conditions"

**Submit:**
- Click **"Submit Application"**
- You'll see a confirmation message
- Application status will show as **"Pending"**

### Document Upload Tips

- Ensure documents are clear and well-lit
- All text should be readable
- Keep documents within file size limits (5MB max)
- Use JPG or PNG for photos
- Use PDF for official documents
- Check file format before uploading (JPG, JPEG, PNG, or PDF only)

### Expected Timeline

- **Submission:** Instant
- **Bank Review:** 2-5 business days
- **Status Check:** View in your dashboard
- **Decision:** Check your dashboard for approval or rejection

## Managing Your Accounts

### Viewing Your Bank Accounts

1. From your dashboard, click **"Bank Accounts"** in the sidebar
2. All your created accounts will be displayed
3. Accounts show with bank name, type, status, and other details

### Filtering Your Accounts

**Status Filter Options:**
- **All Accounts** - Shows all your accounts regardless of status
- **Pending Review** - Applications currently being reviewed by banks
- **Approved** - Applications approved and ready to use
- **Rejected** - Applications that were declined

**Refresh Accounts:**
- Click the **Refresh** button (↻ icon) to manually refresh the account list
- Shows current status of all applications
- Useful after submitting a new application

### Account Card Information

Each account card displays:
- 🏛️ Bank name
- 🌍 Country
- 🏢 Branch
- 📅 Creation date
- Status badge (Pending/Approved/Rejected)
- 💳 Account number (if approved)

### Viewing Full Account Details

1. Click **"View Full Details"** button on any account card
2. A modal window opens showing:
   - All submitted information (personal, educational, income, nominee details)
   - Account status
   - Rejection reason (if application was rejected)
   - List of all 4 uploaded documents
   - Download buttons for each document

### Downloading Documents

1. Click **"View Full Details"** on the account card
2. In the modal, scroll to **"Documents"** section
3. You'll see 4 documents:
   - ID Proof
   - Address Proof
   - Income Proof
   - Passport Photo
4. Click **"Download"** next to the document you want
5. File saves to your computer's default download location
6. File name is the original filename you uploaded

### If Application is Rejected

- Check the rejection reason in your dashboard
- Common reasons:
  - Documents unclear or illegible
  - Information mismatch or incomplete
  - ID or address proof requirements not met
  - Expired or invalid documents

**To Reapply:**
1. You can create another application for the same bank
2. Correct any issues identified
3. Resubmit with updated information

   - Username
   - Phone number

### Admin Dashboard

After login as admin, you'll see:

- Quick navigation tabs (Pending, Approved, Rejected)
- Admin profile information
- Admin settings

### Viewing Applications

1. From the admin dashboard, click the tab for:
   - **Pending** - Applications waiting for review
   - **Approved** - Already approved applications
   - **Rejected** - Rejected applications

2. Each application shows:
   - Applicant name
   - Application date
   - Account type requested

### Reviewing an Application

1. Click on an application to open it
2. Review all sections:
   - Personal Details (name, DOB, contact info)

   - Income Information
   - Nominee Details
   - Account Type and Deposit Amount

3. Review all 4 uploaded documents:
   - ID Proof (check validity and clarity)

### Approving an Application

1. Review all details and documents
2. If everything is correct and complete:
   - Click **"Approve"** button
3. Application status changes to "Approved"
4. Application moves to Approved tab
5. Applicant can see approval in their dashboard

### Rejecting an Application

1. If there are issues:
   - Click **"Reject"** button
2. (Optional) Add a rejection reason explaining what needs correction
3. Application status changes to "Rejected"

1. Click on your profile/settings
2. View your information:
   - Username
   - Email
   - Bank Name

   - Phone Number

3. To edit:
   - Click **"Edit"** button
   - Update username or email
   - Click **"Save"**

4. To change password:
   - Click **"Change Password"**
   - Enter current password

   - Click **"Update"**

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**"I forgot my password"**
- Unfortunately, the app doesn't have an automated password reset
- Contact support with your registered email
- They will verify your identity and help you reset

**"Can't log in with my email"**
- Ensure you're using the email you signed up with
- Check for typos
- Verify CAPS LOCK is off
- Try clearing browser cache and cookies

### Account Creation Issues

**"Upload button won't work"**
- Check file format (must be JPG, JPEG, PNG, or PDF)
- Check file size (must be under 5MB)
- Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
- Clear browser cache and try again

**"I submitted incorrect information"**
- If application is still pending:
  - Contact support with your email and account details
  - They may be able to help correct it

- If application was rejected:
  - You can submit a new application with corrected information

**"Documents were rejected"**
- Documents must be clear and readable
- All text must be visible
- Documents must not be expired
- Address on documents should match your application address
- Resubmit with better quality documents

### Application Status Issues

**"Why is my application still pending?"**
- Normal processing time: 2-5 business days
- Admin is reviewing your documents
- Check your dashboard regularly for updates
- Status updates automatically

**"Why was my application rejected?"**
- Check your dashboard for the rejection reason
- Common issues: unclear documents, incomplete information, missing fields
- Resubmit with corrections

**"I can't see my bank in the list"**
- Ensure you selected the correct country
- Try searching by exact bank name
- Make sure we support your country (India, USA, UK only)
- Contact support if your bank should be available

### Profile Issues

**"I can't update my profile"**
- New email must be unique (not already used)
- Click the edit button (pencil icon)
- Ensure all fields are filled
- Click "Save Changes"

**"Profile changes didn't save"**
- Check that there are no validation errors (red messages)
- Try logging out and back in
- Refresh the page and try again

### Technical Issues

**"Page won't load"**
- Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Clear cache and cookies

**"Server error message"**
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- The backend server may be temporarily down
- Contact support if issue persists

**"Theme/dark mode not saving"**
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Make sure JavaScript is enabled
- Try logging out and back in

### Common Questions

**Q: How long does it take to approve my account application?**
A: Account application processing typically takes 1-3 business days depending on the bank and country. You'll receive comprehensive status updates throughout the review process.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Yes! You can create accounts with different banks and in different countries. All accounts appear in your dashboard under "Bank Accounts" section.

**Q: What documents do I need to open a bank account?**
A: Documentation requirements vary by country and bank type. For Indian accounts, you typically need Aadhaar card, PAN card, and address proof. For USA/UK accounts, passport and local address verification may be required. Our platform guides you through the specific requirements for each bank.

**Q: How many accounts can I create?**
A: There is no limit. Create as many as you need across different banks and countries.

**Q: What happens if my application is rejected?**
A: If your application is not approved, check the rejection reason in your account details. You can contact support for assistance and reapply once you've corrected the identified issues.

**Q: Can I delete an account?**
A: Account deletion is not currently supported through the interface. Contact support if you need to remove an account.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. The application uses:
- SSL encryption for all data
- Secure password hashing
- Proper session management
- Authentication tokens

**Q: What happens after approval?**
A: After your application is approved:
1. Status changes to "Approved" in your dashboard
2. You can download your submitted documents
3. You can view your complete account details
4. You can use your account according to bank policies

**Q: Can I reapply after rejection?**
A: Yes! You can submit a new application for the same bank. Make sure to correct the issues that caused the rejection based on the feedback provided.

**Q: How do I check my application status?**
A: You can monitor all applications in real-time through the "Bank Accounts" section of your dashboard. Filter by status (Pending, Approved, Rejected) to see your applications. Status updates automatically when admins review your application.
