# InterBankHub - Admin Guide

Complete guide for bank administrators using InterBankHub.

---

## What is an Admin?

An Admin (Bank Administrator) is a staff member of a bank who manages account applications from customers. Admins review, approve, or reject account applications on behalf of their bank.

**Admin vs User Roles:**
- **User:** Customer who creates bank accounts
- **Admin:** Bank staff who processes and approves accounts

---

## Getting Started as an Admin

### Step 1: Sign Up as Admin

1. Go to home page and click "Sign Up"
2. Select "Admin" as account type
3. Fill in your information:
   - Email address
   - Password (strong password recommended)
   - Confirm password
   - Username
   - Phone number
   - **Bank Name** (select from available banks)
   - **Country** (India, USA, or UK)
4. Click "Sign Up"
5. You'll be redirected to Sign In Page

### Step 2: Log In to Admin Dashboard

1. Click "Sign In"
2. Enter email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll see the Admin Dashboard

---

## Admin Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard has a left sidebar with four sections:

**Sidebar Sections:**
1. **My Profile** - View your info, edit details, change password, logout
2. **Dashboard** - Overview stats and quick actions
3. **Applications** - Review, approve, and reject account applications
4. **New Branch** - Create a new branch for your bank

**Theme Toggle:**
- A Light/Dark mode toggle is at the bottom of the sidebar
- Your theme preference is saved automatically

**Key Information displayed:**
- Your bank name
- Your admin username
- Number of pending, approved, rejected, and total applications

---

## Managing Applications

### View All Applications

1. **Click "Applications" in the sidebar**
2. **Each application card shows:**
   - Applicant name
   - Current status badge (Pending / Approved / Rejected)
   - Email
   - Country
   - Bank name

### Filter Applications

Use the **"Filter by Status"** dropdown:
- **Pending** - Applications waiting for your decision
- **Approved** - Successfully processed applications
- **Rejected** - Applications that did not meet requirements
- **All Applications** - Show all applications at once

### View Application Details

1. Click the **"View Details"** button (eye icon) on any application card
2. A modal opens showing:
   - Applicant name
   - Email
   - Country
   - Bank name
   - Current status
3. Click outside the modal or the close button to dismiss it

---

## Reviewing an Application

### Step 1: Open Application Details

1. Go to **Applications** in the sidebar
2. Find the application you want to review
3. Click the **"View Details"** button (eye icon)
4. A modal shows: applicant name, email, country, bank, and status

### Step 2: Make a Decision

For **Pending** applications, two action buttons appear on the application card:
- **Approve** button - to approve the application
- **Reject** button - to reject the application

A confirmation dialog will appear before either action is applied.

---

## Making a Decision

### Approve an Application

**When to approve:**
- All documents are clear and valid
- Information is consistent and complete
- Income is sufficient for account type
- No red flags or inconsistencies
- All requirements met

**To approve:**
1. Go to Applications and find the Pending application
2. Click the **"Approve"** button on the application card
3. Confirm in the dialog that appears
4. The application status updates to "Approved" immediately

---

### Reject an Application

**When to reject:**
- Documents are unclear or incomplete
- Information is inconsistent or doesn't match
- Income insufficient for account type
- Documents expired or invalid
- Required information is missing
- Red flags detected

**To reject:**
1. Go to Applications and find the Pending application
2. Click the **"Reject"** button on the application card
3. Confirm in the dialog that appears
4. The application status updates to "Rejected" immediately

**Common rejection reasons:**
- "Documents are not clear or readable"
- "Address on ID does not match provided address"
- "Aadhaar/SSN/NIN is invalid or expired"
- "Income is insufficient for this account type"
- "Missing required documents"
- "Information is inconsistent"
- "Employment verification failed"

## Change Your Password

### Steps to Change Password

1. **Click "My Profile" in the sidebar**
2. **Click "Change Password" button**
3. A modal titled "Reset Password" opens
4. **Enter your current password**
5. **Enter your new password** (must be different from current)
6. **Confirm new password** (must match exactly)
7. **Click "Update Password"**
8. On success you'll see: "Password updated successfully!"

### Password Requirements

- Minimum 8 characters
- Must contain letters and numbers
- Should contain special characters for security
- Cannot be the same as your current password

### Tips for Strong Passwords

✅ Use a mix of uppercase and lowercase
✅ Include numbers (0-9)
✅ Include special characters (@, #, $, !, &, etc.)
✅ Make it at least 12 characters for better security
✅ Don't use easily guessable words
✅ Don't share your password

## Adding a New Branch

Admins can create new branch locations for their bank.

### Steps to Add a Branch

1. **Click "New Branch" in the sidebar** (or click "Add New Branch" from the Dashboard)
2. Click the **"Add New Branch"** button on the page
3. A modal titled "Add New Branch" opens with the following fields:
   - **Country** - Auto-filled from your admin profile (read-only)
   - **City** - Enter the city name (required)
   - **Bank Name** - Auto-filled from your admin profile (read-only)
   - **Branch Name** - Enter a name for the branch (required)
   - **Branch Code** - Enter a unique code (minimum 3 characters, required)
4. Click **"Create Branch"**
5. On success you'll see: "Branch created successfully!"

**Note:** Country and Bank Name are automatically filled from your admin account and cannot be changed.

## Application Workflow

### The Complete Process:

1. **Application Submitted**
   - Customer submits account application
   - Status: "Pending"
   - Admin sees it in dashboard

2. **Review & Verification** (Your role)
   - You review documents
   - You verify information
   - You make decision

3. **Decision Made** (Your action)
   - **APPROVE:** Application approved
   - **REJECT:** Application rejected with reason

4. **Customer Notified**
   - Customer receives email about decision
   - For approval: Customer can now use account
   - For rejection: Reason provided, can resubmit

5. **Record Kept**
   - Approved applications stay in "Approved" section
   - Rejected applications in "Rejected" section
   - You can always review past decisions

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Clear Application

**Situation:** All documents are clear, information is complete and consistent

**Action:**
- All information matches
- Income is sufficient
- No inconsistencies
- Documents are valid

**Decision:** APPROVE ✓

---

### Scenario 2: Unclear Documents

**Situation:** Photos are blurry or incomplete

**Action:**
- Cannot verify document authenticity
- Information cannot be confirmed
- Risk of fraud

**Decision:** REJECT (Reason: "Documents are not clear or readable")

---

### Scenario 3: Address Mismatch

**Situation:** Address on ID doesn't match application address

**Action:**
- Addresses must match for verification
- Could indicate fraud or error
- Cannot proceed without matching addresses

**Decision:** REJECT (Reason: "Address mismatch between documents")

---

### Scenario 4: Insufficient Income

**Situation:** Stated income is too low for account type

**Action:**
- Check bank's minimum income requirements
- Current account typically requires higher income
- Savings account has lower requirements

**Decision:** REJECT (Reason: "Income insufficient for this account type")

---

### Scenario 5: Missing Information

**Situation:** One field was left blank

**Action:**
- Application is incomplete
- Cannot process without all information
- Need to request resubmission

**Decision:** REJECT (Reason: "Missing required information")

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't see all applications"
- Refresh your browser
- Log out and log back in
- Try a different browser
- Check if you're viewing the right status filter

### "Documents are not loading"
- Try a different browser
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Check your internet connection

### "I accidentally approved/rejected an application"
- Contact support immediately: noreplyinterbankinghub@gmail.com
- Explain the situation
- They may be able to help correct it

### "Application status is not updating"
- Refresh the page
- Wait a few seconds (status updates may take time)
- Log out and log back in
- Contact support if issue persists
