# InterBankHub - Admin Guide

Complete guide for bank administrators using InterBankHub.

---

## What is an Admin?

An Admin (Bank Administrator) is a staff member of a bank who manages account applications from customers. Admins review, approve, or reject account applications on behalf of their bank.

**Admin vs User Roles:**
- **User:** Customer who creates bank accounts
- **Admin:** Bank staff who processes and approves accounts

---

## Getting Started as an Admin

### Step 1: Sign Up as Admin

1. Go to home page and click "Sign Up"
2. Select "Admin" as account type
3. Fill in your information:
   - Email address
   - Password (strong password recommended)
   - Confirm password
   - Username
   - Phone number
   - **Bank Name** (select from available banks)
   - **Country** (India, USA, or UK)
4. Click "Sign Up"
5. You'll be redirected to Sign In Page

### Step 2: Log In to Admin Dashboard

1. Click "Sign In"
2. Enter email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll see the Admin Dashboard

**All statuses are important:**

## Reviewing Documents

### View Uploaded Documents

1. **In the application detail page, scroll to "Documents" section**
2. **You'll see 4 uploaded documents:**
   - ID Proof
   - Address Proof
   - Income Proof
   - Passport Photo

### Check Each Document

For each document, verify:

**ID Proof:**
- ✓ All fields readable
- ✓ Photo is clear
- ✓ Signature visible
- ✓ Not expired
- ✓ Matches applicant's name
- ✓ Country appropriate (Aadhaar for India, SSN for USA, Passport/License for USA/UK, etc.)

**Address Proof:**
- ✓ Clearly shows address
- ✓ Document is recent (within 3-6 months)
- ✓ From recognized source (utility company, government, bank, etc.)
- ✓ Applicant's name visible
- ✓ Complete address shown
- ✓ Matches address on ID

**Income Proof:**
- ✓ Shows salary/income amount
- ✓ Recent (within 3 months)
- ✓ From recognized employer or institution
- ✓ Matches stated occupation
- ✓ Income matches or exceeds stated income
- ✓ Clear and readable

**Passport Photo:**
- ✓ Applicant clearly visible
- ✓ Recent photo
- ✓ Professional quality
- ✓ Plain background
- ✓ No sunglasses or extreme expressions
- ✓ Matches applicant name

## Making a Decision

### Approve an Application

**When to approve:**
- All documents are clear and valid
- Information is consistent and complete
- Income is sufficient for account type
- No red flags or inconsistencies
- All requirements met

**To approve:**
1. Review all information and documents
2. Make sure everything is in order
3. Click "Approve" button
4. Confirm your action
5. Application is approved
6. Customer receives approval notification

---

### Reject an Application

**When to reject:**
- Documents are unclear or incomplete
- Information is inconsistent or doesn't match
- Income insufficient for account type
- Documents expired or invalid
- Required information is missing
- Red flags detected

**To reject:**
1. Review all information and documents
2. Identify the reason for rejection
3. Click "Reject" button
4. You may optionally add a rejection reason/note
5. Confirm your action
6. Application is rejected
7. Customer receives rejection notification with details

**Common rejection reasons:**
- "Documents are not clear or readable"
- "Address on ID does not match provided address"
- "Aadhaar/SSN/NIN is invalid or expired"
- "Income is insufficient for this account type"
- "Missing required documents"
- "Information is inconsistent"
- "Employment verification failed"

## Change Your Password

### Step 1: Go to Security Section

1. **Go to Admin Dashboard**
2. **Click on "Change Password" or "Security" option**

### Step 2: Enter Passwords

1. **Enter your current password**
2. **Enter your new password** (must be different from current)
3. **Confirm new password** (must match exactly)
4. **Click "Update"**

### Password Requirements

- Minimum 8 characters
- Must contain letters
- Must contain numbers
- Should contain special characters for security
- Cannot be same as previous password

### Tips for Strong Passwords

✅ Use mix of uppercase and lowercase
✅ Include numbers (0-9)
✅ Include special characters (@, #, $, !, &, etc.)
✅ Make it at least 12 characters for better security
✅ Don't use easily guessable words
✅ Don't share your password

## Application Workflow

### The Complete Process:

1. **Application Submitted**
   - Customer submits account application
   - Status: "Pending"
   - Admin sees it in dashboard

2. **Review & Verification** (Your role)
   - You review documents
   - You verify information
   - You make decision

3. **Decision Made** (Your action)
   - **APPROVE:** Application approved
   - **REJECT:** Application rejected with reason

4. **Customer Notified**
   - Customer receives email about decision
   - For approval: Customer can now use account
   - For rejection: Reason provided, can resubmit

5. **Record Kept**
   - Approved applications stay in "Approved" section
   - Rejected applications in "Rejected" section
   - You can always review past decisions

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Clear Application

**Situation:** All documents are clear, information is complete and consistent

**Action:**
- All information matches
- Income is sufficient
- No inconsistencies
- Documents are valid

**Decision:** APPROVE ✓

---

### Scenario 2: Unclear Documents

**Situation:** Photos are blurry or incomplete

**Action:**
- Cannot verify document authenticity
- Information cannot be confirmed
- Risk of fraud

**Decision:** REJECT (Reason: "Documents are not clear or readable")

---

### Scenario 3: Address Mismatch

**Situation:** Address on ID doesn't match application address

**Action:**
- Addresses must match for verification
- Could indicate fraud or error
- Cannot proceed without matching addresses

**Decision:** REJECT (Reason: "Address mismatch between documents")

---

### Scenario 4: Insufficient Income

**Situation:** Stated income is too low for account type

**Action:**
- Check bank's minimum income requirements
- Current account typically requires higher income
- Savings account has lower requirements

**Decision:** REJECT (Reason: "Income insufficient for this account type")

---

### Scenario 5: Missing Information

**Situation:** One field was left blank

**Action:**
- Application is incomplete
- Cannot process without all information
- Need to request resubmission

**Decision:** REJECT (Reason: "Missing required information")

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't see all applications"
- Refresh your browser
- Log out and log back in
- Try a different browser
- Check if you're viewing the right status filter

### "Documents are not loading"
- Try a different browser
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Check your internet connection

### "I accidentally approved/rejected an application"
- Contact support immediately: support@interbankshub.com
- Explain the situation
- They may be able to help correct it

### "Application status is not updating"
- Refresh the page
- Wait a few seconds (status updates may take time)
- Log out and log back in
- Contact support if issue persists

## Security & Confidentiality

### Important Reminders:

🔒 **Protect Applicant Data**
- Never share customer information
- Keep passwords secure
- Log out when leaving your desk
- Don't leave account open unattended

🔒 **Verify Before Acting**
- Double-check before approving/rejecting
- Ensure documents are authentic
- Never approve based on incomplete info
- Take your time to review carefully

🔒 **Follow Procedures**
- Always verify identity of person submitting
- Keep records of your decisions
- Report suspicious applications
- Follow your bank's policies
