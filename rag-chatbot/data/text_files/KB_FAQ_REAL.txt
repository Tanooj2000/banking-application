# InterBankHub - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Getting Started

**Q: What is InterBankHub?**
A:
InterBankHub is a multi-country banking platform that allows you to create and manage bank accounts across India, USA, and UK. You can browse banks, create accounts through a streamlined digital process, and manage all your accounts from a unified dashboard.

*Also looking for: What does InterBankHub do, Platform overview, Banking platform*

---

**Q: How do I get started with InterBankHub?**
A:
1. Visit the home page
2. Click "Sign Up" to create an account
3. Choose your account type (User or Admin)
4. Fill in your details (email, password, phone number, country)
5. Verify your account
6. Log in and explore banks or manage accounts

A:
**Savings Account:**
- For personal use
- Earn interest on balance
- Limited transactions
- Lower fees

**Current Account:**
- For business use
- Unlimited transactions
- No interest

- Bulk payment options

## Account Creation

**Q: How do I create a bank account?**
A:
1. Log in to your user account
2. Go to "User Dashboard" click New Account Section
3. Select Country, City and Bank as per availability
4. Click "Create Account" for your chosen bank
5. Complete a 6-step form:
   - Select Branch
   - Personal Details
   - Educational Details
   - Income Details
   - Nominee Details
   - Upload Documents
5. Review and submit
6. Wait for approval (typically 2-5 business days)

*Also looking for: Open account, New account, Create account, Bank account setup*

---

**Q: How long does account creation take?**
A:
Typical timeline:
- Documents submitted: Instant
- Bank verification: 2-5 business days
- Account approval: Status updates in your dashboard

The exact time may vary depending on bank processing and document completeness. Check your dashboard regularly for status updates.

*Also looking for: How long, Processing time, Account approval time, Verification timeline*

---

**Q: What documents do I need to upload?**
A:
Required documents for all countries:
- ID Proof (national ID, passport, license, etc.)
- Address Proof (utility bill, bank statement, government letter)
- Income Proof (salary slip, tax return, bank statement)
- Passport-sized Photo
- Consent form (checkbox in the form)

Specific requirements vary by country:
- **India:** Aadhaar Number and PAN Number
- **USA:** Social Security Number (SSN)
- **UK:** National Insurance Number (NIN)

*Also looking for: Document requirements, What ID needed, Required documents, Documents to upload*

---

**Q: Can I create multiple accounts?**
A:
Yes! You can create multiple accounts across different banks and countries. Each account is linked to your profile for easy management.

*Also looking for: Multiple accounts, Add more accounts, Create another account*

## User Account Management

**Q: How do I view my bank accounts?**
A:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Your Page" or Dashboard
3. You'll see all your linked bank accounts
4. You can filter by account status (All, Pending, Approved, Rejected)
5. Click on any account to view details

*Also looking for: View accounts, My accounts, Account list*

---

**Q: How do I edit my profile?**
A:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Your Page" (User Page)
3. Click on the "Profile" section (or edit icon)
4. Update your information:
   - Username
   - Email
   - Phone Number
5. Click "Save Changes"

*Also looking for: Update profile, Edit profile, Change details*

---

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Your Page"
3. Look for "Change Password" or "Security" section
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter your new password (must be different from current)
6. Confirm your new password
7. Click "Update"

*Also looking for: Reset password, Change password, New password*

---

**Q: How do I download documents from my account?**
A:
1. Go to "Your Page"
2. Click on the account you want to access
3. Look for "Documents" or "View Documents"
4. Select the document you want to download
5. Click "Download" button
6. File will save to your computer

*Also looking for: Download files, Get documents, Export documents*

## For Admins

**Q: I'm a bank admin, how do I sign up?**
A:
1. Click "Sign Up"
2. Select "Admin" as your account type
3. Fill in your details:
   - Email
   - Password
   - Username
   - Phone Number
   - Select your Bank Name
   - Select your Country
4. Click "Sign Up"
5. You'll be redirected to the Admin Dashboard

*Also looking for: Admin signup, Bank admin account, Staff account*

---

**Q: What can I do in the Admin Dashboard?**
A:
As an admin, you can:
- View all account applications
- Filter by status: Pending, Approved, Rejected
- View complete account details and submitted documents
- Approve or Reject account applications
- Add notes or feedback to applications
- Edit your admin profile (email, username)
- Change your password

*Also looking for: Admin features, Admin dashboard, Manager tools, What can admins do*

---

**Q: How do I approve an account application?**
A:
1. Log in to your admin account
2. Go to Admin Dashboard
3. View the "Pending" applications
4. Click on the application you want to review
5. Check all the documents and details
6. Click "Approve" button if everything is correct
7. The user will receive an approval notification

*Also looking for: Approve account, Accept application, Process application*

---

**Q: How do I reject an account application?**
A:
1. Log in to your admin account
2. Go to Admin Dashboard
3. View the "Pending" applications
4. Click on the application
5. Review the documents and details
6. Click "Reject" button if there are issues
7. You may optionally provide feedback
8. The user receives a rejection notification with details

*Also looking for: Reject account, Decline application, Deny account*
